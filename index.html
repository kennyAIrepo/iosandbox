Spatial computing
with nothing to install.

Today spatial experiences mean a headset, a download, a wall of cameras. h0p3 collapses that into a link. The webcam already on every phone and laptop becomes a full-body, full-hand tracking rig — and the page renders effects, physics and avatars in real time, in the browser.

For studios and agencies running 20+ camera rigs for a single shot, that's the whole stack in a tab: capture, render, publish. For everyone else, it's the first spatial layer you can reach without leaving the web.
